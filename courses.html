Columbus College

+2 Science

This program is designed for students interested in pursuing higher education in science-related fields such as engineering, medicine, and research. It provides a strong foundation in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: SLC or equivalent
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: National Examination Board (NEB)

+2 Management

This program is suitable for students interested in pursuing careers in business, management, and entrepreneurship. It covers subjects like Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, and Mathematics, providing a solid foundation for future studies and career opportunities.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: SLC or equivalent
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: National Examination Board (NEB)

BBM (Bachelor of Business Management)

BBM is an undergraduate program that focuses on various aspects of business management, including finance, marketing, human resources, and organizational behavior. It prepares students for leadership roles in the corporate world.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: Intermediate or equivalent
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University (TU)

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

BCA is a professional undergraduate program that focuses on computer applications and software development. It covers topics such as programming languages, database management, software engineering, and web development.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: Intermediate or equivalent with Mathematics
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University (TU)

BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies)

BBS is an undergraduate program that provides a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of business and management. It covers subjects like accounting, finance, economics, marketing, and organizational behavior.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: Intermediate or equivalent
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University (TU)

BASW (Bachelor of Arts in Social Work)

BASW is an undergraduate program that focuses on social work theories, practices, and interventions. It prepares students for careers in various social service sectors, including community development, counseling, advocacy, and policy analysis.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: Intermediate or equivalent
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University (TU)

MBS (Master of Business Studies)

MBS is a postgraduate program that provides advanced knowledge and skills in business management. It is suitable for individuals seeking career advancement or specialization in areas such as finance, marketing, human resources, and international business.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Admission Process: Entrance exam and interview
  • Affiliation: Tribhuvan University (TU)
